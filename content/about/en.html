--- title: Introduction tags: ['about', 'en'] --- `TheSLinux` is an open source Linux distribution. The first generation of `TheSLinux` is based on [ArchLinux][]. To keep in touch with the project please use the [mailing lists](/lists/en/), our [Twitter channel][Twitter] or our [Facebook Fanpage][Facebook]. The source code of `TheSLinux` is available on [Github][].
